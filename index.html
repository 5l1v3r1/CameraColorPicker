Camera Color Picker

Camera Color Picker is a free and open source Android app that lets you capture, in real time, the colors around you using the camera of your device.

Camera color picker is out!

We are glad to annonce that Camera Color Picker is finally available on the Google play store. You can download the app for free at https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=fr.tvbarthel.apps.cameracolorpicker

Beta Test

Even if the app is released, we are still adding some feature to Camera Color Picker. The new functionnalities are always tested on the beta channel before hitting production. So if you are interedted in helping us improving our little project, follow this few steps to enter the beta tests:

  • Step 1: join our G+ community
  • Step 2: become a test of the app Camera Color Picker (Note: if you did not join the G+ community, you will find a 404 error. See step 1.)
  • Step 3: download the app from here (Note: if you are not a beta tester, you will find a 404 error. See step 2.

We are also very interested in any suggestions, remarks or observations. Please, feel free to send us an email: tvbarthel [at] gmail.com or leave a message here =).

Version 0.0.5 (2015-05-17)

  • New feature: you can now combine the colors to create beautiful palette.

  • New feature: you can now activate the flash of the device's camera.
  • Add a dialog to confirm the deletion of a color.

  • Add a subtle message when a color is successfully saved.

  • Start beta testing.
  • Use the in-apps of the Play Store for donation purpose.
  • Add a product flavor for the Google Play Store.
  • Add a product flavor for the F-droid store.

Version 0.0.1 (2015-02-21)

  • Capture and save the colors with your device camera.
  • List all the colors saved.
  • Copy the html code of a color to the clipboard.
