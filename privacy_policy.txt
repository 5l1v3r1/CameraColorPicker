Welcome to the Camera Color Picker Privacy Policy

Personal data:
The app does not collect any personnal information.

Camera usage:
The camera is only used for picking a color:
the color is extracted from the pixels at the
center of the camera preview.

If you have a doubt about what the app is doing, feel
free to browse the code source: the app is 100% open source
and has nothing to hide =)
